Engineering & Manufacturing Services

NASMA International provides end-to-end development and white-label manufacturing for advanced medical devices. Our services are designed for clients who require absolute discretion, rigorous quality control, and technological innovation.

Integrated Health Monitoring Systems

Full-cycle development and production of remote monitoring solutions. From wearable biosensors for continuous patient data streaming to stationary platforms for automated mass screening in high-traffic facilities. We engineer the hardware and software for seamless, secure data acquisition and integration.

  • Custom wearable & stationary device design
  • Real-time data analytics & cloud integration
  • Multi-parameter biosensing capabilities
  • Secure data transmission protocols

Longevity and Bio-Augmentation Devices

Engineering of non-invasive systems utilizing targeted light, electromagnetic, and acoustic technologies for therapeutic and enhancement applications. Devices are engineered for both clinical environments and private use.

  • Non-invasive cellular stimulation technologies
  • Customizable treatment protocols
  • Clinical & consumer device design
  • Biocompatible materials & safety systems

Advanced Mobility and Support Exoskeletons

Full-cycle production of powered exoskeletons for medical rehabilitation, industrial logistics, and emergency response. Our engineering priorities are actuator performance, energy efficiency, and intuitive human-machine interface (HMI) integration.

  • Medical rehabilitation exoskeletons
  • Industrial load-support exosuits
  • Emergency response mobility systems
  • Advanced actuator & battery systems

Custom Medical Hardware Solutions

High-volume manufacturing of sophisticated medical devices requiring complex electronic and mechanical integration. We specialize in transforming client specifications into reliable, user-ready products manufactured to scale.

  • Complex electronic assembly (PCBA)
  • High-volume manufacturing capacity
  • Precision mechanical integration
  • Supply chain management

A Methodical Approach to Manufacturing

Our operational framework is designed for precision and confidentiality. Each project undergoes a structured lifecycle—from initial feasibility assessment and proprietary design to rigorous prototyping, production, and validation—ensuring the outcome meets the exacting standards required by our governing body and clientele.

  1. Feasibility Assessment - Technical and commercial evaluation
  2. Prototype Development - Functional prototype creation
  3. Testing & Validation - Rigorous quality assurance testing
  4. Manufacturing - Scalable production implementation
  5. Quality Assurance - Continuous quality monitoring
  6. Delivery & Support - White-label packaging and distribution
